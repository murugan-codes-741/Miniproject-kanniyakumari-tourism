About Kanniyakumari

The Land of Three Seas

Kanniyakumari is deeply rooted in history, spirituality, and breathtaking natural beauty. It is the only place in India where you can watch the sunrise and sunset from the exact same spot. For centuries, it has been a beacon for travelers, saints, and poets.

Our mission is to bring the magic of the southernmost tip of India to the world. Step into the rich heritage, feel the ocean breeze, and explore the monuments in a stunning virtual space.

  • 🌊 Meeting point of Bay of Bengal, Arabian Sea & Indian Ocean
  • 🏛 Rich ancient architecture & Tamil heritage
  • ✨ Spiritual legacy of Swami Vivekananda

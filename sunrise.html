The Most Sacred Sunrise in India

Kanniyakumari is one of the few places on Earth where you can witness both sunrise and sunset over the sea. Every morning, thousands of pilgrims and tourists gather at the shoreline to witness the breathtaking moment when the sun emerges from the meeting point of three great oceans. On full moon days, you can even see the moonset and sunrise simultaneously — a once-in-a-lifetime experience.

Traveler Tips

  • Arrive by 5:00 AM to get a good spot along the viewing gallery.
  • Carry a light shawl — the ocean breeze can be unexpectedly cool.
  • Best viewed from the main shore beach near the Cape.
  • Full moon day sunrise is an extra special event.
  • Avoid the peak monsoon season (June–September) for clear views.
